Lumba Sumba Pass Trek

Overview

  • Destination: Lumba Sumba Pass (5,177 m)
  • Region: Kanchenjunga to Makalu Region
  • Start Point: Suketar (Taplejung)
  • End Point: Tumlingtar
  • Duration: 20–24 Days
  • Difficulty: Challenging
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Fly Kathmandu ➝ Bhadrapur ➝ Drive to Taplejung
  • Day 2: Trek Taplejung ➝ Chirwa
  • Day 3: Trek Chirwa ➝ Sekathum
  • Day 4: Trek Sekathum ➝ Amjilosa
  • Day 5: Trek Amjilosa ➝ Gyabla
  • Day 6: Trek Gyabla ➝ Ghunsa
  • Day 7: Acclimatization Day at Ghunsa
  • Day 8: Trek Ghunsa ➝ Khambachen
  • Day 9: Trek Khambachen ➝ Lhonak
  • Day 10: Exploration & Rest Day
  • Day 11: Cross Lumba Sumba Pass ➝ Pass Camp
  • Day 12: Trek Pass Camp ➝ Thudam
  • Day 13: Trek Thudam ➝ Chyamtang
  • Day 14: Trek Chyamtang ➝ Hatiya
  • Day 15: Trek Hatiya ➝ Gola
  • Day 16: Trek Gola ➝ Num
  • Day 17: Drive Num ➝ Tumlingtar
  • Day 18: Fly Tumlingtar ➝ Kathmandu

Lumba Sumba Pass

Highlights

  • One of Nepal’s most remote and least-trekked routes
  • Connects Kanchenjunga and Makalu regions
  • High alpine passes and glacier crossings
  • Authentic Tibetan & Rai cultures
  • True expedition-style trekking experience
