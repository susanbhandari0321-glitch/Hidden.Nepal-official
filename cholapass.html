Cho La Pass Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Cho La Pass (5,420 m)
  • Region: Everest (Khumbu)
  • Start Point: Lukla
  • End Point: Lukla
  • Duration: 15–18 Days
  • Best Season: Spring (Mar–May) & Autumn (Sep–Nov)

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Fly from Kathmandu ➝ Lukla ➝ Trek to Phakding
  • Day 2: Trek from Phakding ➝ Namche Bazaar
  • Day 3: Acclimatization day at Namche Bazaar
  • Day 4: Trek from Namche ➝ Tengboche
  • Day 5: Trek from Tengboche ➝ Dingboche
  • Day 6: Acclimatization day at Dingboche
  • Day 7: Trek from Dingboche ➝ Lobuche
  • Day 8: Trek to Everest Base Camp ➝ Gorakshep
  • Day 9: Hike to Kala Patthar ➝ Dzongla
  • Day 10: Cross Cho La Pass (5,420 m) ➝ Thagnak
  • Day 11: Trek from Thagnak ➝ Gokyo
  • Day 12: Hike Gokyo Ri ➝ Explore Gokyo Lakes
  • Day 13: Trek from Gokyo ➝ Dole
  • Day 14: Trek from Dole ➝ Namche Bazaar
  • Day 15: Trek from Namche ➝ Lukla
  • Day 16: Fly back to Kathmandu

Cho La Pass View

Highlights

  • Challenging crossing of Cho La Pass (5,420 m)
  • Visit Everest Base Camp and climb Kala Patthar
  • Explore the stunning turquoise Gokyo Lakes
  • Panoramic Himalayan views: Everest, Lhotse, Nuptse, Ama Dablam
  • True high-altitude adventure for experienced trekkers
