Panch Pokhari Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Panch Pokhari Trek
  • Start Point: Dhunche
  • End Point: Dhunche
  • Max Altitude: Panch Pokhari (~4,100 m)
  • Duration: 9 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn
  • Region: Langtang National Park

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu ➝ Dhunche
  • Day 2: Trek from Dhunche ➝ Thade Khola
  • Day 3: Trek from Thade Khola ➝ Thuman
  • Day 4: Trek from Thuman ➝ Panch Pokhari
  • Day 5: Explore Panch Pokhari (sacred lakes and surroundings)
  • Day 6: Trek from Panch Pokhari ➝ Thuman
  • Day 7: Trek from Thuman ➝ Thade Khola
  • Day 8: Trek from Thade Khola ➝ Dhunche
  • Day 9: Drive from Dhunche ➝ Kathmandu

Panch Pokhari Lakes

Highlights

  • Visit five sacred alpine lakes considered holy by Hindus and Buddhists.
  • Beautiful Langtang mountain views and peaceful natural surroundings.
  • Experience local Tamang and Sherpa culture and monasteries.
  • Less crowded and off-the-beaten-path trek.
  • Rich biodiversity in Langtang National Park including rhododendron forests.
