Manaslu Circuit Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Manaslu Circuit Trek (5,106 m)
  • Start Point: Machha Khola
  • End Point: Dharapani
  • Duration: 12 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu ➝ Machha Khola
  • Day 2: Trek from Machha Khola➝ Jagat
  • Day 3: Trek from Jagat➝ Deng
  • Day 4: Trek from Deng➝ Namrung
  • Day 5: Trek from Namrung➝ Sama Gaun
  • Day 6: Trek from Sama Gaun➝ Samdo
  • Day 7: Trek from Samdo➝Dharmasala
  • Day 7: Trek from Dharmasala ➝ Bhimtang via Larke La Pass (5,160m)
  • Day 9: Trek from Bhimtang ➝ Dharapani
  • Day 9: Return Drive to Kathmandu via Besisahar

Manaslu Circuit Photo

Highlights

  • The Manaslu Circuit Trek is an advanced-level trek that takes you around the stunning Manaslu peak, one of the highest peaks in the world.
  • The trek offers stunning views of the Himalayan mountains, including Manaslu, Ganesh Himal, and Annapurna, as well as the opportunity to experience the local Gurung and Tibetan cultures.
  • Pine and rhododendron forests
  • Off-the-beaten-path Himalayan experience
