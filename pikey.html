Pikey Peak Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Pikey Peak Trek (4,065 m)
  • Start Point: Dhap
  • End Point: Phaplu
  • Duration: 7 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn
  • Region: Lower Everest (Solukhumbu)

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu ➝ Dhap (2,900 m)
  • Day 2: Trek from Dhap ➝ Jhapre (2,820 m)
  • Day 3: Trek from Jhapre ➝ Pikey Base Camp (3,640 m)
  • Day 4: Hike to Pikey Peak Summit (4,065 m) ➝ Trek to Loding
  • Day 5: Trek from Loding ➝ Junbesi (2,700 m)
  • Day 6: Explore Junbesi & Thupten Chholing Monastery
  • Day 7: Trek to Phaplu ➝ Drive or Fly back to Kathmandu

Pikey Peak Photo

Highlights

  • 360° panoramic sunrise views of Everest, Makalu, Kanchenjunga, and Annapurna ranges.
  • Less crowded and peaceful alternative to the classic Everest trails.
  • Cultural experiences in Sherpa villages and ancient monasteries like Thupten Chholing.
  • Ideal for trekkers seeking a short yet rewarding Himalayan experience.
  • Forests of rhododendrons, yak pastures, and alpine landscapes.
