Upper Dolpo Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Upper Dolpo (Shey Phoksundo National Park)
  • Region: Far-Western Nepal
  • Start Point: Juphal
  • End Point: Jomsom / Juphal
  • Max Elevation: 5,190 m (Kang La Pass)
  • Duration: 20–25 Days
  • Best Season: May–October

Itinerary (Sample – 22 Days)

  • Day 1: Fly Kathmandu ➝ Nepalgunj ➝ Juphal
  • Day 2: Trek Juphal ➝ Dunai
  • Day 3: Trek Dunai ➝ Chhepka
  • Day 4: Trek Chhepka ➝ Jharana Hotel
  • Day 5: Trek to Ringmo (Phoksundo Lake)
  • Day 6: Acclimatization at Phoksundo Lake
  • Day 7: Trek Ringmo ➝ Chunuwar
  • Day 8: Trek Chunuwar ➝ Snowfields Camp
  • Day 9: Cross Kang La Pass (5,190 m) ➝ Shey Gompa
  • Day 10: Explore Shey Gompa (Crystal Mountain)
  • Day 11: Trek Shey Gompa ➝ Namgung
  • Day 12: Trek Namgung ➝ Saldang
  • Day 13: Trek Saldang ➝ Yangze Gompa
  • Day 14: Trek Yangze ➝ Sibu
  • Day 15: Cross Sela La Pass ➝ Tokyu
  • Day 16: Trek Tokyu ➝ Dho Tarap
  • Day 17: Rest day at Dho Tarap
  • Day 18: Trek Dho Tarap ➝ Tarap Khola
  • Day 19: Trek Tarap Khola ➝ Khanigaon
  • Day 20: Trek Khanigaon ➝ Dunai
  • Day 21: Trek Dunai ➝ Juphal
  • Day 22: Fly back to Kathmandu

Upper Dolpo Landscape

Highlights

  • Remote trans-Himalayan landscape and Tibetan culture
  • Shey Phoksundo Lake – Nepal’s deepest and most beautiful lake
  • Ancient monasteries: Shey Gompa, Yangze Gompa, Namgung
  • High mountain passes over 5,000 m
  • One of the most restricted and least-explored trekking regions in Nepal

Permits Required

  • Upper Dolpo Restricted Area Permit
  • Shey Phoksundo National Park Permit
  • Minimum 2 trekkers with licensed guide required
