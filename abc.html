Annapurna Base Camp (ABC) Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Annapurna Base Camp (4,130 m)
  • Region: Annapurna, Kaski District
  • Duration: 8–10 Days
  • Start/End Point: Pokhara
  • Best Season: March–May & September–November

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara ➝ Nayapul ➝ Trek to Ghandruk
  • Day 2: Ghandruk ➝ Chhomrong (2,170 m)
  • Day 3: Chhomrong ➝ Bamboo (2,310 m)
  • Day 4: Bamboo ➝ Deurali (3,230 m)
  • Day 5: Deurali ➝ Annapurna Base Camp (4,130 m)
  • Day 6: ABC ➝ Bamboo
  • Day 7: Bamboo ➝ Jhinu Danda (hot springs)
  • Day 8: Jhinu ➝ Nayapul ➝ Drive to Pokhara

Highlights

  • Close-up view of Annapurna I (8,091 m), Machapuchare & Hiunchuli
  • Charming villages like Ghandruk & Chhomrong
  • Natural hot springs at Jhinu Danda
  • Lush forests, waterfalls, and Himalayan wildlife
  • Teahouse trekking with diverse landscapes
