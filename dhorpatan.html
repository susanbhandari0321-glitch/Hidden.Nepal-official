Dhorpatan Trek

Overview

  • Region: Western Nepal (Baglung, Rukum, Myagdi)
  • Duration: 7–10 days
  • Max Altitude: ~3,900 m
  • Start Point: Beni or Baglung
  • Best Season: Spring (Mar–May) & Autumn (Sep–Nov)
  • Difficulty: Moderate

Itinerary (7 Days)

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara to Beni ➝ Darbang
  • Day 2: Trek to Takam (1,675 m)
  • Day 3: Trek to Lamsung (2,200 m)
  • Day 4: Trek to Dhorpatan (2,870 m)
  • Day 5: Exploration in Dhorpatan Hunting Reserve
  • Day 6: Trek back to Gurjaghat
  • Day 7: Trek to Beni and drive back to Pokhara

Dhorpatan Reserve

Highlights

  • Only hunting reserve in Nepal
  • Remote Tibetan-influenced villages
  • Alpine meadows and dense forests
  • Home to Blue Sheep, Himalayan Tahr, and rare birds
  • Peaceful and off-the-beaten-path experience
