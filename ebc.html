Everest Base Camp Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Everest Base Camp (5,364 m)
  • Highest Point: Kala Patthar (5,545 m)
  • Duration: 12 Days (Lukla–EBC–Lukla)
  • Start/End: Lukla
  • Best Seasons: March–May & September–November

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Fly to Lukla (2,860 m) ➝ Trek to Phakding (2,610 m)
  • Day 2: Phakding ➝ Namche Bazaar (3,440 m)
  • Day 3: Acclimatization at Namche Bazaar (Hike to Everest View Hotel)
  • Day 4: Namche ➝ Tengboche (3,860 m)
  • Day 5: Tengboche ➝ Dingboche (4,410 m)
  • Day 6: Acclimatization at Dingboche (Hike to Nangkartshang Peak)
  • Day 7: Dingboche ➝ Lobuche (4,940 m)
  • Day 8: Lobuche ➝ Gorakshep ➝ Everest Base Camp ➝ Gorakshep (5,164 m)
  • Day 9: Hike to Kala Patthar ➝ Pheriche (4,280 m)
  • Day 10: Pheriche ➝ Namche Bazaar
  • Day 11: Namche ➝ Lukla
  • Day 12: Fly back to Kathmandu

Everest Base Camp

Highlights

  • Base of the world’s highest peak – Mt. Everest (8,848.86 m)
  • Namche Bazaar: gateway to Everest region
  • Views of Everest, Lhotse, Nuptse, Ama Dablam
  • Tengboche Monastery – spiritual center of Khumbu
  • Kala Patthar viewpoint – sunrise over Everest
