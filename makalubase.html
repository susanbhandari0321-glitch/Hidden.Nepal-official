Makalu Base Camp Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Makalu Base Camp Trek
  • Start Point: Tumlingtar
  • End Point: Tumlingtar or Tashigaon
  • Max Altitude: Makalu Base Camp (~4,870 m)
  • Duration: 18–20 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn
  • Region: Makalu-Barun National Park, Eastern Nepal

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Fly from Kathmandu to Tumlingtar
  • Day 2: Trek Tumlingtar ➝ Chichila
  • Day 3: Trek Chichila ➝ Num
  • Day 4: Trek Num ➝ Seduwa
  • Day 5: Trek Seduwa ➝ Tashigaon
  • Day 6: Trek Tashigaon ➝ Yangle Kharka
  • Day 7: Trek Yangle Kharka ➝ Yangla Kharka
  • Day 8: Trek Yangla Kharka ➝ Makalu Base Camp
  • Day 9: Explore Makalu Base Camp and surrounding
  • Day 10-18: Trek back via same route to Tumlingtar
  • Day 19: Fly back to Kathmandu

Makalu Base Camp

Highlights

  • Remote trek through Makalu-Barun National Park with rich biodiversity.
  • Spectacular views of Makalu, Lhotse, Everest, and other peaks.
  • Experience Sherpa and Rai cultures in remote villages.
  • Cross pristine forests, alpine meadows, and glacial rivers.
  • Less crowded and off-the-beaten-path trek for adventurous trekkers.
