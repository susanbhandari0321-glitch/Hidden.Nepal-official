Badimalika Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Badimalika Temple (Badimalika), Bajura, Far West Nepal
  • Start Point: Martadi / Kolti (access via Dhangadhi or Nepalgunj by road or flight + jeep/drive)
  • Max Elevation: ~4,200–4,214 m (Badimalika Temple ridge)
  • Duration: Typically 7–15 days depending on route and pace (many itineraries: ~8–12 days common)
  • Best Season: Spring (Mar–May) and Autumn (Sep–Nov)
  • Region: Bajura / Sudurpashchim Province (far-west Nepal)

(Facts above compiled from trekking guides and operator itineraries.) :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Suggested Itinerary (example — 9 days)

  • Day 1: Fly/Drive to Dhangadhi or Nepalgunj → Road/jeep to Martadi
  • Day 2: Drive/jeep to trailhead (e.g., Jadga/Karala) → Start trek
  • Day 3: Trek to village (Karala → Budha Krodh)
  • Day 4: Trek toward higher pastures (Bhito / grasslands)
  • Day 5: Ascend toward Badimalika ridge & camp/tented lodge
  • Day 6: Visit Badimalika Temple (pilgrimage & views)
  • Day 7: Begin return trek toward Martadi
  • Day 8: Drive/jeep back to Dhangadhi/Nepalgunj
  • Day 9: Fly/drive to Kathmandu (or continue to other far-west treks)

Note: routes, road access and flights can vary—many operators combine Badimalika with Khaptad or Rara routes.

Badimalika Temple & Surroundings

Highlights

  • Spiritual pilgrimage to the remote Badimalika Bhagwati temple perched on a high ridge.
  • Wide grassy meadows, sweeping hill ridges and panoramic views of Api–Saipal and other western ranges.
  • Traditional far-west Nepal culture, remote villages and seasonal pastoral life.
  • Off-the-beaten-path experience with fewer trekkers and mostly camping/tent sections in upper reaches.

Practical Tips

  • Prepare for limited lodges above certain elevations — camping/tented support often needed.
  • Road and flight schedules in the far west can be weather dependent — allow buffer days.
  • Permits: check with your operator for local permits (area is remote; operators usually handle logistics).
  • Fitness: moderate to strenuous depending on itinerary and camping sections.
