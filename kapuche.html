Kapuche Lake Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Kapuche Lake (2,546 m)
  • Start Point: Pokhara ➝ Sikles
  • End Point: Pokhara
  • Duration: 4–5 Days
  • Best Season: Autumn & Spring

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara ➝ Sikles
  • Day 2: Trek from Sikles ➝ Hugu Goth
  • Day 3: Trek from Hugu Goth ➝ Kapuche Lake ➝ Return to Hugu Goth
  • Day 4: Trek back to Sikles ➝ Drive to Pokhara

Kapuche Lake Photo

Highlights

  • Visit the world’s lowest glacial lake at 2,546 m above sea level.
  • Scenic drive and trek through traditional Gurung villages like Sikles.
  • Panoramic views of Annapurna II, Lamjung Himal, and Machhapuchhre (Fishtail).
  • Opportunity to experience authentic rural culture and local hospitality.
  • Ideal short trek from Pokhara for adventure and photography lovers.
