Kanchenjunga Base Camp Trek

Overview

  • Region: Eastern Nepal (Taplejung District)
  • Duration: 18–23 days
  • Max Altitude: 5,143 m (Pangpema Base Camp)
  • Start Point: Taplejung (via flight to Bhadrapur + drive)
  • Best Seasons: Spring (Mar–May) & Autumn (Sep–Nov)
  • Difficulty: Challenging

Sample Itinerary (North & South Base Camps)

  • Day 1: Fly Kathmandu ➝ Bhadrapur ➝ Drive to Taplejung
  • Day 2: Taplejung ➝ Mitlung
  • Day 3: Mitlung ➝ Chirwa
  • Day 4: Chirwa ➝ Sekathum
  • Day 5: Sekathum ➝ Amjilosa
  • Day 6: Amjilosa ➝ Gyabla
  • Day 7: Gyabla ➝ Ghunsa
  • Day 8: Acclimatization day at Ghunsa
  • Day 9: Ghunsa ➝ Kambachen
  • Day 10: Kambachen ➝ Lhonak
  • Day 11: Lhonak ➝ Pangpema (Kanchenjunga North Base Camp) ➝ Back to Lhonak
  • Day 12: Lhonak ➝ Ghunsa
  • Day 13: Ghunsa ➝ Sele La
  • Day 14: Sele La ➝ Tseram
  • Day 15: Tseram ➝ Ramche ➝ Oktang (South Base Camp) ➝ Back to Ramche
  • Day 16: Ramche ➝ Tortong
  • Day 17: Tortong ➝ Yamphudin
  • Day 18: Yamphudin ➝ Khebang ➝ Drive to Bhadrapur
  • Day 19: Fly back to Kathmandu

Kanchenjunga Landscape

Highlights

  • Explore both North and South Base Camps
  • Close-up view of Mt. Kanchenjunga (8,586m)
  • Rich biodiversity: Red Panda, Himalayan Thar
  • Traditional Limbu and Rai culture
  • Remote valleys and stunning glacier landscapes
