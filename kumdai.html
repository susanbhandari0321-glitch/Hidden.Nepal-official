Khumai Danda Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Khumai Danda (3,245 m)
  • Start/End Point: Pokhara
  • Duration: 4 Days
  • Best Season: Spring (Mar–May) & Autumn (Sep–Nov)
  • Region: Annapurna Region

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara ➝ Hemja ➝ Trek to Hile Kharka
  • Day 2: Trek from Hile Kharka ➝ Khumai Danda
  • Day 3: Early morning hike to Korchan (3,682 m) ➝ Back to Khumai ➝ Descend to Ghachowk
  • Day 4: Trek to Lumre ➝ Drive back to Pokhara

Khumai Danda View

Highlights

  • Panoramic views of Machhapuchhre (Fishtail), Annapurna, and Mardi Himal
  • Peaceful off-the-beaten-path route, perfect for beginners and solitude seekers
  • Beautiful sunrise from Korchan ridge (3,682 m)
  • Dense rhododendron and oak forests
  • Interaction with Gurung villages and local lifestyle
