Rara Lake Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Rara Lake (2,990 m)
  • Start Point: Jumla
  • End Point: Jumla or Nepalgunj
  • Duration: 12 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Kathmandu ➝ Nepalgunj (Flight)
  • Day 2: Nepalgunj ➝ Jumla (Flight)
  • Day 3: Jumla ➝ Chere Chaur (3,050 m)
  • Day 4: Chere Chaur ➝ Chalachaur (2,980 m)
  • Day 5: Chalachaur ➝ Sinja Valley (2,490 m)
  • Day 6: Sinja ➝ Ghorosingha (3,050 m)
  • Day 7: Ghorosingha ➝ Rara Lake (2,990 m)
  • Day 8: Exploration at Rara Lake (Murma Top Optional)
  • Day 9: Rara ➝ Pina (2,440 m)
  • Day 10: Pina ➝ Bumra (2,850 m)
  • Day 11: Bumra ➝ Jumla
  • Day 12: Jumla ➝ Nepalgunj ➝ Kathmandu

Highlights

  • Rara Lake – the largest lake in Nepal
  • Remote Karnali culture and lifestyle
  • Historical Sinja Valley (ancient Malla kingdom)
  • Pine and rhododendron forests
  • Off-the-beaten-path Himalayan experience
