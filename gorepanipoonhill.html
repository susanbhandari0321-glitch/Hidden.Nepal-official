Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek
  • Start Point: Nayapul (or Tikhedhunga)
  • End Point: Nayapul (via Ghandruk)
  • Max Elevation: Poon Hill (3,210 m)
  • Duration: 5 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn
  • Region: Annapurna Region

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara ➝ Nayapul ➝ Trek to Tikhedhunga or Ulleri
  • Day 2: Trek from Ulleri ➝ Ghorepani (2,860 m)
  • Day 3: Early morning hike to Poon Hill for sunrise ➝ Trek to Tadapani
  • Day 4: Trek from Tadapani ➝ Ghandruk Village
  • Day 5: Trek from Ghandruk ➝ Nayapul ➝ Drive back to Pokhara

Poon Hill Sunrise View

Highlights

  • Stunning sunrise view over Annapurna and Dhaulagiri from Poon Hill.
  • Short and easy trek suitable for families and beginners.
  • Dense rhododendron forests (especially colorful in spring).
  • Traditional Gurung villages like Ghandruk with cultural museums.
  • Great mountain views without high-altitude risks.
  • Accessible from Pokhara with comfortable lodges along the trail.
