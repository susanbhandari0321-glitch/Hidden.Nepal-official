Mardi Himal Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Mardi Himal Base Camp (4,500 m)
  • Region: Annapurna Region, Kaski District
  • Duration: 5–7 Days
  • Start/End Point: Pokhara ➝ Kande or Dhampus
  • Best Season: March–May & September–November

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara ➝ Kande ➝ Trek to Deurali (2,100 m)
  • Day 2: Deurali ➝ Forest Camp (2,600 m)
  • Day 3: Forest Camp ➝ Low Camp (3,150 m)
  • Day 4: Low Camp ➝ High Camp (3,550 m)
  • Day 5: High Camp ➝ Mardi Himal Base Camp (4,500 m) ➝ Return to High Camp
  • Day 6: High Camp ➝ Siding Village (1,700 m)
  • Day 7: Siding ➝ Drive to Pokhara

Highlights

  • Less-crowded, peaceful alternative to ABC
  • Close-up views of Machapuchare and Annapurna South
  • Dense forests, alpine meadows, and rhododendrons
  • Authentic homestay experience in Siding Village
  • Short and rewarding high-altitude trek
