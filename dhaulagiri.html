Dhaulagiri Circuit Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Dhaulagiri Base Camp (4,740 m)
  • Highest Point: French Pass (5,360 m)
  • Duration: 15 Days
  • Region: Western Nepal (Myagdi District)
  • Start/End: Beni ➝ Marpha or Jomsom
  • Best Season: April–May & September–November

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara ➝ Beni
  • Day 2: Beni ➝ Babiachor
  • Day 3: Babiachor ➝ Dharapani
  • Day 4: Dharapani ➝ Muri
  • Day 5: Muri ➝ Boghara
  • Day 6: Boghara ➝ Dobang
  • Day 7: Dobang ➝ Italian Base Camp (3,660 m)
  • Day 8: Acclimatization at Italian Base Camp
  • Day 9: Italian BC ➝ Glacier Camp
  • Day 10: Glacier Camp ➝ Dhaulagiri Base Camp (4,740 m)
  • Day 11: Rest & explore around Dhaulagiri Base Camp
  • Day 12: Cross French Pass (5,360 m) ➝ Hidden Valley
  • Day 13: Cross Dhampus Pass (5,240 m) ➝ Yak Kharka
  • Day 14: Yak Kharka ➝ Marpha (join Annapurna Circuit)
  • Day 15: Drive or fly from Jomsom ➝ Pokhara

Dhaulagiri Himal

Highlights

  • World’s 7th highest peak (8,167 m)
  • Off-the-beaten-path high-altitude adventure
  • Remote Magar and Gurung villages
  • Challenging passes: French & Dhampus
  • Stunning glaciers, hidden valleys, and alpine scenery
