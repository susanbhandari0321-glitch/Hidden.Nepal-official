Annapurna Circuit Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Annapurna Circuit Trek
  • Start Point: Besisahar
  • End Point: Jomsom / Pokhara
  • Max Elevation: Thorong La Pass (5,416 m)
  • Duration: 14–18 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn
  • Region: Annapurna Region

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu ➝ Besisahar ➝ Bhulbhule
  • Day 2: Trek from Bhulbhule ➝ Jagat
  • Day 3: Trek from Jagat ➝ Dharapani
  • Day 4: Trek from Dharapani ➝ Chame
  • Day 5: Trek from Chame ➝ Pisang
  • Day 6: Trek from Pisang ➝ Manang
  • Day 7: Acclimatization Day in Manang (Optional hike to Ice Lake or Gangapurna)
  • Day 8: Trek from Manang ➝ Yak Kharka
  • Day 9: Trek from Yak Kharka ➝ Thorong Phedi
  • Day 10: Cross Thorong La Pass (5,416 m) ➝ Muktinath
  • Day 11: Trek from Muktinath ➝ Jomsom via Kagbeni
  • Day 12: Fly or drive to Pokhara
  • Day 13: Drive/Fly back to Kathmandu

Highlights

  • Cross the world's highest trekking pass: Thorong La (5,416 m).
  • Diverse landscapes: from lush valleys to arid high mountain terrain.
  • Spectacular views of Annapurna, Dhaulagiri, Machhapuchhre, and Tilicho Peak.
  • Visit Muktinath, a sacred temple for both Hindus and Buddhists.
  • Rich culture in Tibetan-influenced Manang and Lower Mustang.
  • Option to visit Tilicho Lake, one of the world's highest lakes.
