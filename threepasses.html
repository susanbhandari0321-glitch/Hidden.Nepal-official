Everest Three Passes Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Everest Three Passes Trek
  • Start Point: Lukla
  • End Point: Lukla
  • Duration: 18 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn
  • Max Altitude: Kongma La (5,535 m), Cho La (5,420 m), Renjo La (5,360 m)

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Fly from Kathmandu ➝ Lukla, Trek to Phakding
  • Day 2: Trek from Phakding ➝ Namche Bazaar
  • Day 3: Acclimatization Day in Namche (Hike to Everest View Hotel)
  • Day 4: Trek from Namche ➝ Tengboche
  • Day 5: Trek from Tengboche ➝ Dingboche
  • Day 6: Acclimatization Day in Dingboche (Hike to Nagarjun Hill)
  • Day 7: Trek from Dingboche ➝ Chhukung
  • Day 8: Cross Kongma La Pass ➝ Lobuche
  • Day 9: Trek to Gorakshep ➝ Everest Base Camp ➝ Back to Gorakshep
  • Day 10: Hike to Kala Patthar ➝ Trek to Dzongla
  • Day 11: Cross Cho La Pass ➝ Gokyo
  • Day 12: Acclimatization and Explore Gokyo Lakes & Gokyo Ri
  • Day 13: Cross Renjo La Pass ➝ Marlung
  • Day 14: Trek to Namche Bazaar
  • Day 15: Trek to Lukla
  • Day 16: Fly back to Kathmandu

Highlights

  • Challenging high-altitude trek crossing three major passes: Kongma La, Cho La, and Renjo La.
  • Close-up views of Mt. Everest, Lhotse, Makalu, and Cho Oyu.
  • Visit to Everest Base Camp and the stunning Gokyo Lakes.
  • Rich Sherpa culture, monasteries, and remote highland villages.
  • Panoramic viewpoints like Gokyo Ri and Kala Patthar.
  • Less crowded and more adventurous than classic EBC trek.
