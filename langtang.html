Langtang Valley Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Kyanjin Gompa / Langtang Valley (3,870 m)
  • Region: Langtang National Park, Rasuwa District
  • Duration: 7–10 Days
  • Start/End Point: Syabrubesi
  • Best Season: March–May & September–November

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu ➝ Syabrubesi (1,550 m)
  • Day 2: Trek to Lama Hotel (2,380 m)
  • Day 3: Lama Hotel ➝ Langtang Village (3,430 m)
  • Day 4: Langtang ➝ Kyanjin Gompa (3,870 m)
  • Day 5: Acclimatization: Hike to Kyanjin Ri or Tserko Ri
  • Day 6: Kyanjin ➝ Lama Hotel
  • Day 7: Lama Hotel ➝ Syabrubesi
  • Day 8: Drive back to Kathmandu

Highlights

  • Explore Kyanjin Gompa, ancient Buddhist monastery
  • Stunning views of Langtang Lirung, Dorje Lakpa, Yala Peak
  • Cultural experience with Tamang and Tibetan communities
  • Glaciers, yak pastures, and alpine forests
  • Close to Kathmandu – ideal for short Himalayan adventure
