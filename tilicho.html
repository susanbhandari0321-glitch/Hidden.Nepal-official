Tilicho Lake Trek Route

Tilicho Lake Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Tilicho Lake (4,919 m)
  • Region: Annapurna, Manang District
  • Duration: 7–10 Days
  • Start Point: Besisahar
  • End Point: Jomsom or Nayapul
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu ➝ Besisahar ➝ Chame
  • Day 2: Drive from Chame ➝ Pisang (3,200 m)
  • Day 3: Drive From Pisang ➝ Manang (3,540 m)
  • Day 4: Acclimatization Day in Manang
  • Day 5: Drive from Manang ➝Siri Kharka ➝Trek to Tilicho Base Camp (4,140 m)
  • Day 6: Visit Tilicho Lake (4,919 m) ➝ Return to Siri Kharka
  • Day 7: Drive From Siri Kharka ➝ Besisahar
  • Day 8: Drive From Besisahar ➝ Kathmandu

Highlights

  • Tilicho Lake – one of the highest lakes in the world
  • Scenic views of Annapurna & Gangapurna Himal
  • Challenging trails with stunning alpine landscapes
  • Manang's unique Himalayan culture and monasteries
