Dhampus Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Dhampus Village (1,650 m)
  • Region: Annapurna Region
  • Start Point: Pokhara
  • End Point: Pokhara
  • Duration: 2–3 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara ➝ Phedi ➝ Trek to Dhampus
  • Day 2: Sunrise view at Dhampus ➝ Trek to Australian Camp
  • Day 3: Trek to Kande ➝ Drive back to Pokhara

Dhampus Village View

Highlights

  • Easy and short trek suitable for beginners and families
  • Stunning sunrise views of Annapurna South, Hiunchuli, and Machhapuchhre
  • Traditional Gurung village lifestyle and culture
  • Beautiful stone steps, terraced fields, and forest trails
  • Perfect introduction to trekking in Nepal
