Nar-Phu Valley Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Nar-Phu Valley Trek
  • Start Point: Koto
  • End Point: Upper Pisang or Jomsom
  • Max Altitude: Kang La Pass (5,300 m)
  • Duration: 12 Days
  • Best Season: Spring & Autumn
  • Region: Annapurna Region, Nepal

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu ➝ Besisahar ➝ Koto
  • Day 2: Trek from Koto ➝ Phu Village
  • Day 3: Explore Phu Village (Tibetan Buddhist culture and monastery)
  • Day 4: Trek from Phu ➝ Nar Village
  • Day 5: Explore Nar Village and surroundings
  • Day 6: Trek from Nar ➝ Kang La Base Camp
  • Day 7: Cross Kang La Pass (5,300 m) ➝ Upper Pisang
  • Day 8: Trek from Upper Pisang ➝ Manang
  • Day 9: Acclimatization day in Manang (optional hikes)
  • Day 10: Trek from Manang ➝ Pisang
  • Day 11: Trek from Pisang ➝ Besisahar
  • Day 12: Drive from Besisahar ➝ Kathmandu

Nar-Phu Valley

Highlights

  • Remote and less crowded high-altitude trek with Tibetan culture.
  • Crossing Kang La Pass offering breathtaking panoramic views.
  • Traditional Tibetan villages with monasteries and prayer wheels.
  • Wildlife sightings including blue sheep and Himalayan thar.
  • Combination of stunning mountain landscapes and rich cultural experiences.
  • Opportunity to explore hidden valleys off the main Annapurna Circuit.
