Gosaikunda Lake Trek Route

Overview

  • Destination: Gosaikunda Lake (4,380 m)
  • Region: Langtang National Park
  • Duration: 6–7 Days
  • Start/End Point: Dhunche
  • Best Season: March–May & September–November

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu ➝ Dhunche (1,950 m)
  • Day 2: Trek to Thulo Syabru (2,250 m)
  • Day 3: Thulo Syabru ➝ Lauribina (3,920 m)
  • Day 4: Lauribina ➝ Gosaikunda Lake (4,380 m)
  • Day 5: Exploration around Gosaikunda / Hike to nearby lakes
  • Day 6: Gosaikunda ➝ Dhunche
  • Day 7: Drive back to Kathmandu

Gosaikunda Lake

Highlights

  • Holy alpine lake surrounded by rugged mountains
  • Mythical and religious significance to Hindus and Buddhists
  • Stunning sunrise views from Lauribina
  • Wildlife encounters in Langtang National Park
  • Short yet high-altitude adventure from Kathmandu
