Shey Phoksundo Lake

Overview

  • Region: Dolpa, western Nepal
  • Duration: 18–24 days
  • Max Altitude: 5,190 m (Numa La Pass)
  • Start Point: Juphal (via Nepalgunj)
  • Best Time: May–October
  • Permits: Restricted Area Permit, Shey Phoksundo National Park Permit

Itinerary (18 Days)

  • Day 1: Fly Kathmandu ➝ Nepalgunj
  • Day 2: Fly to Juphal ➝ Trek to Dunai (2,140 m)
  • Day 3: Dunai ➝ Chhepka
  • Day 4: Chhepka ➝ Jharana Hotel
  • Day 5: Jharana ➝ Ringmo Village (Phoksundo Lake)
  • Day 6: Rest Day at Phoksundo Lake
  • Day 7: Phoksundo ➝ Forest Camp (Above Lake)
  • Day 8: Forest Camp ➝ Kang La Base Camp
  • Day 9: Cross Kang La Pass ➝ Shey Gompa (4,400 m)
  • Day 10: Shey Gompa ➝ Namgung
  • Day 11: Namgung ➝ Saldang
  • Day 12: Saldang ➝ Yangze Gompa
  • Day 13: Yangze ➝ Sibu
  • Day 14: Sibu ➝ Jeng La Base
  • Day 15: Cross Jeng La ➝ Tokyu Gaon
  • Day 16: Tokyu ➝ Dho Tarap
  • Day 17: Dho Tarap ➝ Nawarpani
  • Day 18: Nawarpani ➝ Dunai ➝ Juphal

Shey Phoksundo Lake

Highlights

  • Shey Phoksundo Lake – turquoise Himalayan lake
  • Shey Gompa – spiritual heart of Dolpo
  • Crossing high passes: Kang La, Jeng La, Numa La
  • Ancient Bon and Tibetan Buddhist culture
  • Remote and untouched landscapes
  • Yak caravans, blue sheep, and rare snow leopards (if lucky!)
