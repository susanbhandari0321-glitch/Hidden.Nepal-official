Gokyo Valley Trek Route

Overview

  • Region: Everest Region, Khumbu
  • Duration: 11–14 days
  • Max Altitude: 5,357 m (Gokyo Ri)
  • Start Point: Lukla
  • Best Time: March–May & September–November
  • Difficulty: Moderate to challenging

Itinerary (11 Days)

  • Day 1: Fly to Lukla (2,840 m) ➝ Trek to Phakding
  • Day 2: Phakding ➝ Namche Bazaar
  • Day 3: Acclimatization at Namche
  • Day 4: Namche ➝ Dole
  • Day 5: Dole ➝ Machhermo
  • Day 6: Machhermo ➝ Gokyo (4,800 m)
  • Day 7: Hike to Gokyo Ri (5,357 m) ➝ Rest day
  • Day 8: Gokyo ➝ Dole
  • Day 9: Dole ➝ Namche
  • Day 10: Namche ➝ Lukla
  • Day 11: Fly back to Kathmandu

Gokyo Lake View

Highlights

  • Series of turquoise Gokyo Lakes
  • Stunning views from Gokyo Ri: Everest, Lhotse, Makalu, Cho Oyu
  • Less crowded than EBC route
  • Cross Ngozumpa Glacier – Nepal’s largest glacier
  • Rich Sherpa culture in Namche and surrounding villages
